cs2.sh currently supports BUFF, YouPin, C5Game, CSFloat, Skinport, and Steam. All prices are normalized to USD.

| Marketplace | ask | bid | ask_volume | bid_volume | Additional      |
| ----------- | --- | --- | ---------- | ---------- | --------------- |
| BUFF        | Yes | Yes | Yes        | Yes        | -               |
| YouPin      | Yes | Yes | Yes        | Yes        | -               |
| C5Game      | Yes | Yes | Yes        | Yes        | -               |
| CSFloat     | Yes | -   | Yes        | -          | -               |
| Skinport    | Yes | -   | Yes        | -          | max/mean/median, history |
| Steam       | Yes | -   | Yes        | -          | -               |

## Endpoint Notes

### Latest Prices

**Sources:** All 6 marketplaces — BUFF, YouPin, C5Game, CSFloat, Skinport, Steam.

BUFF, YouPin, and C5Game include both ask (sell) and bid (buy) prices with volumes. CSFloat, Skinport, and Steam only have ask prices. Skinport additionally includes `max_ask`, `mean_ask`, `median_ask`, and rolling history objects (`24h_history`, `7d_history`, `30d_history`, `90d_history`).

**Update frequency:** Cache refreshes every 10 seconds. Underlying marketplace data refreshed every ~3 minutes.

### Price History

**Sources:** All 6 marketplaces — BUFF, YouPin, C5Game, CSFloat, Skinport, Steam.

Returns OHLC (Open/High/Low/Close) aggregated data. Each candle records the first, highest, lowest, and last price observed in the interval.

**Intervals and maximum ranges:**

| Interval | Max Range |
|----------|-----------|
| `5m` | 14 days |
| `30m` | 90 days |
| `1h` | 180 days |
| `1d` | 365 days |

**Data available from:** December 24th, 2025 onwards. For longer-term historical data, use [Archive History](/docs/api-reference/archive-history).

### Archive History

**Sources:** `aggregate` (default), `buff`, `youpin`, `c5game`.

The `aggregate` source includes additional fields not available on individual sources: `hourly_volume` and `total_supply`.

**Intervals:** `1h` (hourly), `1d` (daily, default).

**Data range:** ~2-3 years depending on the item. Updates ~1-2 times per day. For recent high-resolution data, use [Price History](/docs/api-reference/prices-history).

### Archive CSFloat

**Source:** CSFloat only. Returns daily sale averages (not live listings).

**Data range:** ~2-3 years depending on the item. Updates ~once per day.

**Fields:** `date` (YYYY-MM-DD), `price` (average sale price USD), `volume` (number of sales that day).

### Liquidity

No source filtering — liquidity is computed across all marketplaces.

**Liquidity rank scale (0-100):**
- 95-100: Very high liquidity
- 85-95: High liquidity
- 70-85: Moderate liquidity
- 60-70: Low liquidity
- Below 60: Very low liquidity

### Health

Public endpoint — no authentication required.

**Status thresholds per source:**
- `up` — Data collected less than 10 minutes ago
- `degraded` — Data collected 10 minutes to 1 hour ago
- `down` — Data collected more than 1 hour ago

Includes `stats` with `total_events`, `market_hash_names`, and `variant_items` counts.
